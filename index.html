about



Muqavil is a startup introduced by the students of G.B Pant govt. engineering college in order to provide good and efficient engineering works to colleges throughout India like medium level construction, electrical works, networking setups. In hope of providing jobs to new and aspiring engineers throughout the country.

We deal in many engineering works like civil,electrical,mechanical,electronics...etc, Aur aim is to provide cheap and best engineering works and provide stable jobs to young and aspiring engineers.
